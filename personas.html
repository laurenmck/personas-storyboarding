Personas & Storyboarding

The goal of this assignment was to step into a users shoes by observing users interacting with an interface, interviewing users about their experiances and illistrating a storyboard for one of my user personas. This assignment consisted of 4 parts: Preparation, Recording Observations, Personas, and Storyboards. I choose to focus in the Brown Univeristy Mail Room Interface for this project

Part 1: Preparation

In part one, I selected an accessable interface - the Brown Univeristy Mail Room Interface, created a sketch and a breif description of the interface and prepared some questions for users regarding their experiances with my choosen interface.

Part 2: Recording Observations

In part two, I observed users interacting with the Brown Mail Room Interface and asked users questions about their experices with this interface.

Part 3: Personas

In part three, I used the info I recieved from speaking with users to create a persona for my interface. I created a singular persona for my interface becuase I felt that was all I needed to sucessful protray the users of the Brown U Mailroom interface.

Part 4: Storyboards

In part four, I created a storyboard to depict a users experiance with the Brown U Mailroom Interface.
